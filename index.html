Triangle is Equilateral:
An Equilateral Triangle has 3 sides of equal length

Triangle is Isosceles:
An Isosceles Triangle has only two sides of equal length

Triangle is Scalene:
A Scalene Triangle has three sides of differing lengths

Triangle is Valid:
A triangle is only valid if the three sides can connect. No side can be longer than the other two combined. If this displays false, then the triangle is impossible, and whether or not it technically qualifies for Scalene or Isosceles is irrelevant.
